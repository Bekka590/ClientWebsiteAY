Anxiety

Understanding Anxiety

Anxiety is a normal response to stress but can become a clinical issue when it interferes with daily life. Our treatment approach is focused on practical, compassionate care.

Symptoms of Anxiety

  • Excessive worry
  • Restlessness or feeling on edge
  • Difficulty concentrating
  • Fatigue or trouble sleeping

Our Approach

We use a combination of therapy, lifestyle strategies and medication (if needed) to support patients through their recovery journey.

Possible Triggers

  • Major life changes
  • Work or academic pressures
  • Trauma or grief
  • Underlying medical conditions
